About

Laguna Creek Makers is a MAKER focused parent group for kids from 3-99 years old located in the Laguna Creek area of Elk Grove, California. We provide our kids opportunities to Make and Learn. We maintain a resource library for Members to use.

We meet monthly and host activities. In the past year we've had activities such as: Tesla Coil Making, Quadcopter Assembly, Duck Tape Wallet Making, Basic Circuits, Introduction to Arduinos, RetroPie, Programming Microbit,...

About Laguna Creek Makers

Laguna Creek Makers is a maker inspired group for Kids 3-18 years old. Parents are encouraged to host STEM focused activities. We meet monthly to participate, exchange ideas, plan, and build.

Engineering

As Engineers, we love to look for inventive solutions to problems. We create solutions using materials we have at hand through planning, building, and testing.

We utilize many different tools to create. 3D Printers, CNC Machines, Laser Cutters, and other tools help us to go from concept to prototype. Knowing how to use these tools in the day and age is integral to our Engineering Skills.
